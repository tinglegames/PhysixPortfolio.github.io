How a commission runs

Brief → build → handoff
01

Brief

You send references, scale, and what the space needs to do. I quote a price and a turnaround.

02

Blockout

Greybox first so layout and scale are agreed before detail time gets spent.

03

Build

Detailing, lighting and set dressing, with progress screenshots as it goes.

04

Handoff

Clean hierarchy, sane names, anchored parts, and hook points your scripter can actually use.
